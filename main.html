Welcome to ZASHED!

Welcome to Zashed, the ultimate math speedrun game where quick thinking and numerical agility are your greatest assets! In Zashed, you'll face a continuous stream of diverse math challenges (gr12) all designed to test your reflexes and mathematical prowess.

Guide on how to play

LOG IN TO PLAY

You firstly need to log in before you are able to play the game, so that you can keep track of your own progress.

OPTIONS

You will be presented with 2 different options after you log in, "simple" and "advanced"(has word problems).

WHITEBOARD

Upon entering the questions, you can press the ▲ to get a whiteboard, which you can write on and ▼ to go back to the answer and submit
